Health Care/Conditions' Relationship with Poverty/Income

Noted Correlations Between Health Risks & Age/Income

This interactive scatterplot can be used to click through multiple variables as a means to analyze their relationships by state. Click through variable combos to explore said relationships.

Insights/Analysis:

  • Limted healthcare availability is strongly correlated with poverty rate. This is particularly prevalent in southern/gulf states.
  • Higher median incomes typically align with healthcare availability.
  • Smoking and obesity are linked to poverty / low income levels.
